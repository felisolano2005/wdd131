Study Abroad Guide for Costa Rican Students

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Study Abroad Guide for Rican Students

This name clearly represents the purpose of the site, which is to help Costa Rican students understand and navigate the process of studying abroad.

Optional domain: cotsaricanstudyabroad.com

Site Purpose

This website provides guidance for Costa Rican students who want to study in another country, especially the United States. It includes information about applications, scholarships, visas, housing, budgeting, and adapting to a new culture. It also includes interactive tools like a cost calculator and a checklist to help students stay organized.

Scenarios

  • What steps do I need to follow to apply to a university in the United States?
  • How can I find scholarships and calculate the total cost of studying abroad?
