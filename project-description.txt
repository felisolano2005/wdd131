Subject

Study Abroad Guide for Rican Students

Content

This website is for Costa Rican students who want to study in another country like the United States.
It will have lots of information. We will talk about what you need to apply how to get scholarships,
what to do for a visa, where to live, how to manage your money and how to adjust to a culture. 
The website will also have some tools that use JavaScript like a calculator to help you figure out 
how much things will cost and a checklist to keep you organized.

Reason

I picked this topic because it matters to me and I think it can really help people. A lot of 
students in Costa Rica want to study but they do not know what to do first. This website can make 
things easier for them. Give them the help they need in a way that is easy to understand. Rican 
students will be able to find everything they need to know about studying abroad in one place. 
The Study Abroad Guide for Rican Students will be a great resource, for them.
